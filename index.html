iParent Allowance Tracking App

I was inspired to do this project by my son. As a busy Mom, I had trouble keeping up with my child's allowance. I promised a dollar a day BUT time would pass and I wouldn't remember when I had paid him OR whether I had purchased something on his behalf with his promise to "pay me back". Plus, there were times when he needed to receive consequences because of his bad behavior and his main incentives are money and screen time. Hence the iParent Allowance Tracker application.

For my purposes, it had to be simple and portable. When my child's behavior needed to be recorded, I couldn't always go to my computer to log it. I can't rely on my memory to recall when my child has spent available funds.

In order to achieve these goals, I built the application on the Ionic Framework, a hybrid mobile application framework that uses the AngularJS platform and Bootstrap to create sleek mobile apps.
